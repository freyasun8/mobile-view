Korean Directors

Park Chan-Wook

Park Chan-wook is one of the most acclaimed Asian filmmakers, both internationally and in his native South Korea. Perhaps his most recognizable work is 2003's Oldboy, which is the second installment in Park's The Vengeance Trilogy. His 2016 erotic psychological thriller The Handmaiden won the Palme d'Or at Cannes.

Bong Joon-Ho

Taking the Oscars by storm, Bong Joon-Ho can't actually be considered a surprise breakout director considering his filmography has grown gradually over the years, directing one great movie after another.
